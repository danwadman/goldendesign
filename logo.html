Guidance

Exclusion Zone

The logo's exclusion zone is equal to half the height of the icon.

Don't
  1. Apply a gradient to the icon or wordmark.
  2. Rotate the logo.
  3. Change the logo colour or tone outside of the WebBased orange.
  4. Resolve the logo in two different colours.
  5. Distort or warp the logo in any way.
  6. Use the wordmark without the icon.
  7. Outline or create a keyline around the logo.
  8. Change the typeface nor recreate or manipulate the wordmark and the icon.
